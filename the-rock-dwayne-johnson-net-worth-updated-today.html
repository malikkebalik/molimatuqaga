The Rock Dwayne Johnson Net Worth (Updated Today)

Publish date: 2024-08-12

Dwayne Johnson, popular by the name “The Rock,” is an actor, retired professional wrestler, and businessman. He has been a successful actor and before that, he was also considered to be one of the greatest wrestlers.

In this article, we will cover the net worth of “The Rock”, his career earnings, popular films, and others. Read the full article below to know everything about him.

The Rock Net Worth

According to Celebrity Net Worth, the estimated net worth of “The Rock” is said to be around $800 million.

The Rock’s career as a wrestler and actor has contributed to his massive net worth primarily. His 30 to 40 percent stake in Teremana Tequila is also a part of his wealth. He could make more than $1 billion from the company. He typically earns nearly $100 million yearly from his acting career and other collaborations.

Earnings

In 2009, The Rock’s net worth was around $30 million and in less than a year the amount exceeded $50 million. He became the world’s wealthiest wrestler in 2012 after his net worth reached $75 million. By the year 2018, his net worth reached $280 million. The Rock earned $90 million for the period between June 2018 and June 2019.

In 2001, Johnson earned $5.5 million for the film titled “The Scorpion King.” The film became successful and grossed over $165 million against a $60-million budget. The amount that he earned from the film made him the highest-paid actor of that time. In 2003, Dwayne was paid $12.5 million to star in the film “The Rundown” whereas the next year he earned $15 million for “Walking Tall.”

In 2018, he made a whopping amount of $20 million for the film “Skyscraper.” Dwayne’s earnings in 2013 reached $43 million whereas $65 million in 2015.

He earned $125 million between June 2017 and June 2018. He charges nearly $4 million for the promotion of his own films. In 2019, he was paid $20 million for the movie titled “Fast & Furious Presents: Hobbs & Shaw.” 

Other Ventures

Dwayne established a company named Seven Bucks Productions with his then-wife Dany Garcia in 2012. Whenever he stars in any film, the company’s digital, creative as well as production team gets engaged in each decision, from script screening to film marketing. Seven Bucks handles his several social media handles, particularly YouTube and Instagram possibly.

Apart from his work in films, he also published his autobiography named “The Rock Says…,” (2000) in which he also served as the co-writer with Joe Layden. The book peaked at number one on The New York Times Best Seller list and continued to top the list for the next few weeks.

He served as the host and producer of the 2013 TNT reality competition series named “The Hero.” The next year he went on to host another TNT reality series named Wake Up Call. He then hosted NBC’s The Titan Games in 2019.

He collaborated with “Under Armour,” an American fitness apparel manufacturer in 2016 to roll out “Project Rock”.

Endorsements

The Rock also landed several collaboration deals with top companies like Ford, Apple, and Under Armour amongst several others.
