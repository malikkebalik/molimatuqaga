Alkeon Capital Management, LLC (New York)

Publish date: 2024-07-13

Alkeon Capital Management, LLC is a large advisory firm based in New York. It manages $6.97 billion of regulatory assets for 23 client accounts. It has been registered with the SEC as an adviser since 2001 and has operated in the jurisdiction of New York.

Alkeon Capital Management provides portfolio management for investment companies and portfolio management for pooled investment vehicles. It doesn't provide financial planning services to its clients. On top of advisory services, the firm doesn't engage in other business activities.

Alkeon Capital Management: assets under management

The assets under management (AUM) of Alkeon Capital Management, LLC is $6.97 billion, all of which is managed in discretionary accounts. The firm's AUM has grown by 842% since the beginning of its operation, and $4.36 billion of the total AUM is attributable to clients outside of the United States.


The firm manages $6.97 billion in discretionary accounts. On average, the discretionary accounts where all decisions are made on behalf of the client hold $303.2 million each.

Fees & Commissions

The advisory firm has several streams of revenue. Clients pay Alkeon Capital Management, LLC for their services and products based on a percentage of assets under management and performance-based fees. Compensation based on assets under management promotes a long-term relationship between a client and the adviser and helps align mutual interests. These fees don’t include brokerage commissions, interest, taxes, and other account expenses.

What kind of clients Alkeon Capital Management serves?

The firm provides services to investment companies and pooled investment vehicles. The largest group of clients of Alkeon Capital Management is pooled investment vehicles with 22 clients and $4.66 billion of assets under management. Among the 13 most similar advisory firms, Alkeon Capital Management has the 6th lowest percentage of pooled investment vehicles out of all clients.


The amount of regulatory assets under management is $6.97 billion for 23 clients. 57% of the firm’s clients are foreign persons or businesses.

Alkeon Capital Management also advises several private funds. Namely, twelve hedge funds and three venture capital funds. The minimum investment commitment required of non-related persons ranges from $250,000 to $5,000,000.

Careers at Alkeon Capital Management, LLC

The company employs 51 people that are either part- or full-time employees. 35 percent of the employees work as investment advisers or researchers. No employees are registered as investment adviser representatives with the state securities authorities.

There are 24 people with the role of looking for and bringing in new clients on behalf of the firm. The firm doesn’t compensate employees in addition to the salary for bringing in new clients. Employees don’t receive compensation from other entities for client referrals.
