Who is Ariana Fletcher?

Ariana Fletcher who was born on July 12, 1995 is an American social media celebrity, model and businesswoman. Also known as Ari Fletcher, she is famous for her therealkylesister Instagram page.

Who are Ariana Fletcher's parents?

The Instagram sensation was born and raised in Chicago, Illinois. Her mother, Erin, worked as a waitress. Ariana's father, whose name she hasn't shared, was a janitor in high school. She wasn't very close to her dad and rarely talked about him. When the star was 18 years old, her older brother, Kyle, passed away from an illness. The siblings were very close, and Ariana often posts tributes to her brother on social media. Her Instagram handle is dedicated to her brother's memory as well. Ariana also has a sister named Ashley.

Instagram career

Ariana started her Instagram career in 2015, when she decided to use the platform to promote her hair extensions brand. Slowly, but steadily, she grew a large fan base and started posting selfies, fashion and modeling shots. Ariana has endorsed the fast fashion brand Fashion Nova. She also had a contract with Savage X Fenty, but lost it because of her statement about domestic violence. In 2021, the star appeared on the cover of Vogue. She is also available on YouTube and Twitter.

Who's Ariana Fletcher's boyfriend?

The influencer is reportedly dating rap artist Moneybagg Yo. Their relationship was surrounded by several controversies, and Ariana once referred to them as "abusive and toxic". Previously, she was in a relationship with another rapper, G Herbo. The couple was in an on-and-off relationship for several years and have a son named Yosohn, who was born in 2018. After their break-up, Ariana was in a brief relationship with boxer Gervonta Davis.

How much is Ariana Fletcher's net worth?

Ariana Fletcher's net worth is estimated at approximately 10 million dollars as of 2022.
