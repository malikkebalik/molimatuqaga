What is Gary Allan's Net Worth?

Gary Allan is an American country music artist who has a net worth of $10 million. Gary Allan was born in La Mirada, California in December 1967. Allan signed with Decca Records in 1996. His debut studio album Used Heart for Sale was released in 1996 and was certified Gold in the United States. From 1999 to 2003 Alan had three albums in a row go Platinum in the US.

His 2005 album Tough All Over reached #1 on the US Country and #3 on the Billboard 200 charts. Allan's 2013 album Set You Free reached #1 on both the Billboard 200 and US Country charts. He has had #1 hits on the US Country charts with the singles "Man to Man", "Tough Little Boys", "Nothing On but the Radio", and "Every Storm (Runs Out of Rain)" which also was certified Platinum in the US. He was nominated for the Country Music Association's Horizon Award in 2003. Allan is a vocalist who also plays guitar.
