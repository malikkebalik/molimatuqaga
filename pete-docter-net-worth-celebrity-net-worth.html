What is Pete Docter's Net Worth?

Pete Docter is an American film director, screenwriter, animator, producer, and voice actor who has a net worth of $5 million. Pete Docter was born in Bloomington, Minnesota in October 1968. He is associated with Pixar Animation Studios. Docter has written the movies Toy Story, Toy Story 2, Monsters, Inc., WALL-E, Up, and Inside Out.

He has also directed the films Monsters, Inc., Up, and Inside Out. Docter has provided voice work for the movies Monsters, Inc., The Incredibles, Wall-E, Up, and Inside Out. He has served as an executive producer on the films Brave and Monsters University.  Docter has won 30 awards including an Academy Award for Up and BAFTA Awards for Up and Monsters, Inc.
