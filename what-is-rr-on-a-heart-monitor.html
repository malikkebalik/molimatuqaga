The time between beats is measured in milliseconds (ms) and is called an “R-R interval” or “inter-beat interval (IBI).” While heart rate focuses on the average beats per minute, heart rate variability (HRV) measures the specific changes in time (or variability) between successive heart beats.What is a good resting heart rate by age?For adults 18 and older, a normal resting heart rate is between 60 and 100 beats per minute (bpm), depending on the person’s physical condition and age.
