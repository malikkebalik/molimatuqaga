Angelina Jolies Middle-Finger Tattoo Transformation

Publish date: 2024-09-26

No matter what Angelina Jolie wears for the season, her extensive tattoo collection is still visible beneath her coats and jackets. It was recently reported that she has added additional tattoos to her huge collection, this time on her middle fingers.

Famous New York tattoo artist Mr. K, who works at the A-list favorite Bang Bang, posted a shot on Instagram, blurring out the artwork for the time being and teasing a revelation in the near future. Many people assumed that the new ink had to do with Jolie’s divorce from Brad Pitt after seeing a photograph of her open hands with two blurry bars over her middle fingers.

But Mr. K was quick to set the record straight, saying:

“It’s NOTHING related with Brad Pitt,” and promised to upload a picture of the actual tattoo “very soon.”
