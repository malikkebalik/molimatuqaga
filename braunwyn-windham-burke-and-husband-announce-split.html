Braunwyn Windham-Burke and Husband Announce Split

Publish date: 2024-09-14

There’s been a lot of changes in Braunwyn Windham-Burke‘s life lately. She was recently let go from The Real Housewives of Orange County, which reignited drama with Kelly Dodd (who blames Braunwyn for their firing).

On a more personal level, Braunwyn has been on a journey of her own. After getting sober, the reality star acknowledged that she was struggling with her sexuality and identity. She eventually came out as a lesbian but made it known that she and her husband had no plans of divorcing, even though she began dating someone else. Although it’s unconventional, it doesn’t mean it couldn’t work!

But the couple must have been struggling more than they made public–just yesterday they announced that they decided to part ways.
