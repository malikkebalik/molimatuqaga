What is King Hassan II's Net Worth?

King Hassan II was the King of Morocco and had a net worth of $1 billion dollars. King Hassan II was born in Rabat, Morocco in July 1929 and passed away in July 1999. He earned a law degree from the University of Bordeaux. In 1953 he and his father Sultan Mohammed V were exiled to Corsica. In 1957 Hassan was named Crown Prince and became King in 1961 when his father passed away. His conservative rule was characterized by a poor human rights record. He survived two assassination attempts in the early 1970s.

Hassan was a member of the Alaouite dynasty. He allied Morocco with the United States in the Cold War era. He adopted a market-based economy that centered on tourism, agriculture, and phosphates. King Hassan II passed away on July 23, 1999 at the age of 70. He died from natural causes and was buried in the Mausoleum of Mohammed V. He had five children with his wife Lalla Latifa Hammou and none with his wife Lalla Fatima bint Qaid Ould Hassan Amhourak, who he both married in 1961.
