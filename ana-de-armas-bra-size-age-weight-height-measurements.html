Biography:

Ana de Armas was born on 30 April 1988 in Santa Cruz del Norte, Cuba. When she was 12, she made the decision to become an actress. In her later life, she trained at the National Theater School of Cuba. She made her acting debut in the film Una rosa de Francia. By the time she was 18 she relocated to Spain. There, she became known for starring on the Spanish show El Internado.

In 2014, she decided to further career by going to Los Angeles. She was then cast for a role in the movie Knock Knock opposite Keanu Reeves. She collaborated with the actor again in the 2016 movie Exposed. She later starred in the films Hands of Stone, War Dogs, and Overdrive. It was announced she joined the cast of the movie Blade Runner 2049.
