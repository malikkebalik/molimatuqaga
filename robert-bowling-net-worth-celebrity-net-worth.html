What is Robert Bowling's Net Worth?

Robert Bowling is an American video game designer who has a net worth of $400 thousand dollars. Robert Bowling is part of a key team at Infinity Ward, the company that developed popular video game series "Call of Duty", and its subsequent sequels. Bowling is currently serving as Infinity Ward's Creative Strategist, even as many members of the original team have left the company due to a dispute with Activision, their parent company.
