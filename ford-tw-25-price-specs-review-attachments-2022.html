Ford TW-25 Row-Crop Tractor was equipped with a Ford turbocharged diesel 6-cylinder liquid-cooled 401 ci [6.6 L] engine, which delivers 156 hp [116.3 kW] power at rated rpm 2200.

Ford TW-25 Row-Crop Tractor had a Ford 6.6L 6-cyl diesel engine, 20 Qts [18.9 L] engine oil capacity, 4×2 2WD/4×4 MFWD 4WD, 7150 lbs [3243 kg] Rear lift (at 24″/610mm), 12267 to 15500 pounds weight, hydrostatic power steering, 16 forward and 4 reverse gears.

Ford TW-25 Row-Crop tractor attachments include Ford 773 front-end loader.
