Who is Echo Marvel?

Echo (Maya Lopez) is a fictional character appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. She is a superhero from Native America who is deaf and can mimic any move or ability she observes. The Kingpin, a criminal boss and Daredevil foe, adopted her and ordered her to assassinate him.

She later discovered the truth about her father’s passing, though and changed sides to support and fall in love with Daredevil. She also momentarily took on the persona of Ronin, an Avengers member and masked vigilante.

The character has been portrayed as a supporting figure for Daredevil. In the Marvel Cinematic Universe, Alaqua Cox plays Maya Lopez.

She made her TV debut in Hawkeye in 2021 and will soon be appearing in Echo, a spin-off series and below you will read about it.
