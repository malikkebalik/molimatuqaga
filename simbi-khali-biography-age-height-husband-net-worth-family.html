Simbi Khali Social Network

Timeline

Born in Jackson, Mississippi, Khali was raised in Chicago, the youngest of seven children. She started singing at a young age before moving on to acting and attended the Duke Ellington School of the Arts. She then continued her education at the California Institute of the Arts where she graduated with a degree in acting. Khali was previously married to actor Cress Williams from 2000 until 2011 and they have two children: son Elijah Khali Thomas Williams (born July 20, 2004) and daughter Amika Khali Williams (born August 13, 2007).

From 1993 to 1997, she played Sheneneh's vocal best friend in the FOX sitcom Martin. From 1996 to 2001, she played Nina Campbell in the NBC sitcom 3rd Rock from the Sun, where she was initially a recurring character but was bumped to a main character in season 3.

Simbi Khali, sometimes credited as Simbi Kali Williams, (born April 28, 1971) is an American actress, best known for her role as Nina Campbell on the NBC sitcom 3rd Rock from the Sun.
