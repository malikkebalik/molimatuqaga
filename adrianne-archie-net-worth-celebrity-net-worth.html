What is Adrianne Archie's Net Worth?

Adrianne Archie is a Christian singer-songwriter and musician who has a net worth of $4 million dollars. Adrianne Archie was born in Louisville, Kentucky. As a child she sang in church, plays, and talent shows. She graduated with a degree in marketing. Her debut studio album HTHAELHH (He That Hath An Ear, Let Him Hear) was released in 2006.

The album won Archie the Best New Artist and Best Female Artist awards from the Urban Gospel Alliance. In 2009 she released HSMS (Heart, Soul Mind & Strength). In 2009 she received her Masters of Arts in Christian Education. Archie's musical influences include hip-hop, R&B, gospel, and neo-soul. Her album Warm Winter was released in 2006.
