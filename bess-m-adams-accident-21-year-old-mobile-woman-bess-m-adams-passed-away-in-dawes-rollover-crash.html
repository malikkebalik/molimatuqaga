Bess M. Adams Accident; 21 Year Old Mobile Woman, Bess M. Adams Passed Away In Dawes Rollover Crash Alabama

Late on Wednesday night, a woman from Mobile was killed after her vehicle went off the road and flipped over, as reported by the Alabama Law Enforcement Agency (ALEA).

At around 11:47 p.m. on Wednesday, the collision took place on Dawes Road, which is located approximately four miles south of the city boundaries of Mobile. Bess M. Adams, who was 21 years old at the time, was driving a 2020 Nissan Kicks when it went off the road, collided with a culvert, and flipped over.

According to ALEA, Adams was thrown out of the vehicle for not wearing a seat belt at the time of the accident. She passed away shortly after being taken to University Hospital in Mobile, where she was sent after being evacuated there.

An investigation into the cause of the accident is still ongoing, and the ALEA has not disclosed any additional information.
