What Is Jennie From BLACKPINKs Favorite Color?

Publish date: 2024-09-26

Jennie, Jisoo, Lisa, and Rosé make up the K-pop group, BLACKPINK, collaborating with artists like Dua Lipa, Lady Gaga, and Cardi B in the process. With a color-themed band name, it was only natural that Jennie shared two of her favorite colors with fans. 

Here’s what we know about the rapper behind “Solo,” Jennie. 

Jennie is one of the members of BLACKPINK, performing songs like ‘How You Like That’

These idols are in your area. As one of BLACKPINK’s rappers, Jennie appeared on songs like “How You Like That,” “Pretty Savage,” and “Lovesick Girls.” She earned awards and recognition, along with members like Jisoo, Rosé, and Lisa. 

Outside of her work with this K-pop group, Jennie released the first solo song of any BLACKPINK member. That was “Solo,” which premiered in 2018, as did the corresponding music video. 

When it comes to some of Jennie’s favorite colors, though, this idol confirmed that fans never have to guess which are her two favorite shades. (Of course, her favorites are black and pink — the two colors that make up this girl group’s name.)

What is Jennie from BLACKPINK’s favorite color?

Jennie is pretty obvious regarding her favorites, whether that be beauty products or foods. Her favorite colors are also pretty obvious. During one V Live, Jennie revealed why fans should never ask about her favorite colors. They should already know the answer.

“‘What is your favorite color?’ Guys, that’s like the most basic question that you guys should already know about me. Because it’s black and,” Jennie paused and pointed to her hot pink-colored shirt. “I don’t even have to answer.”

Just because black and pink are two of Jennie’s favorite colors, this artist often plays with different color palettes with her outfits. As the unofficial “Queen of Chanel,” Jennie appears as an ambassador for this fashion house, posing in unique garments and sharing her love for fashion with BLINKS. 

BLACKPINK’s Rosé often shares her love for baby pink

BLACKPINK Fandom states the alleged favorite colors of the idols in this K-pop group. That includes the rapper Lisa, whose current favorite color is yellow. Reportedly, the singer Jisoo’s favorite color is purple.

Other members are pretty open about their love of pink, especially Rosé. To her, there’s only one shade that stands out as the best color. The singer even cried when someone brought her the wrong pink shade on set. 

“This is pink. That is what’s most important,” Rosé said while holding up a hairbrush during one video, according to a fan translation. “This is pink. Baby pink. Baby pink is the prettiest pink. It’s not hot pink. It’s baby pink.”

Fans can catch up with Jennie via social media platforms like Instagram and V Live. Music by BLACKPINK is available on most major music streaming platforms.
