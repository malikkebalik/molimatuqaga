What is DJ Khalil's Net Worth?

DJ Khalil is an American hip hop artist and producer who has a net worth of $8 million. DJ Khalil, also known as Khalil Abdul-Rahman, was born in Los Angeles, California, and is most widely recognized for producing multiple hit tracks for various artists, including Dr. Dre and 50 Cent. He also produced multiple tracks for Eminem on his successful albums, "Recovery" and "The Marshall Mathers LP 2". He works with multiple hip hop groups, including The New Royales, which is made up of Khalil, Liz Rodrigues, Erik Alcock and Pranam 'Chin' Injeti.

The group has produced and written tracks for a range of artists and is known for pulling from multiple music traditions when writing tracks. He is also one half of Self Scientific. He is known for incorporating a range of instruments into the tracks he produces and writes. He also regularly uses space effects, cymbals, and analog instruments such as electric guitars.
