Lucas Black Workout Routine

Publish date: 2024-07-18

Hollywood actor Lucas Black has an acting career that spans decades. He first debuted in the 1994 film The War. Afterward, he starred as Caleb Temple in the television series American Gothic. The rest of the ’90s saw him performing in the films Sling Blade, Ghosts of Mississippi, Flash, The X-Files, Our Friend, Martin, and Crazy in Alabama.

He also guest-starred in an episode of Chicago Hope. In the 2000s, he first starred in the television movie The Miracle Worker. He then had performances in several films, including All the Pretty Horses, Cold Mountain, Killer Diller, Friday Night Lights, Deepwater, Jarhead, and Get Low. Black also played the role of Sean Boswell in the action film The Fast and the Furious: Tokyo Drift. In the 2010s, he played Rick Calucci in CSI: Miami.

He also played supporting roles in the films Seven Days in Utopia, Legion, Promised Land, and 42. He then reprised his role as Sean Boswell in Furious 7. Black is also widely known for starring as Special Agent Christopher Lasalle in NCIS: New Orleans, a role he played from 2014 to 2019. To add, he’ll play Sean again in the upcoming Fast and Furious sequel F9.

Lucas Black stays in shape by following simple workout routine. He’s not much of a gym-goer. Instead, he keeps his body fit and strong by being outdoors a lot. When he’s not working, he prefers spending time with his family. He also spends some time outdoors fishing and hunting. While he visits the gym from time to time, the actor doesn’t focus on performing rigorous workouts. He likes to take it easy and enjoy his physical activities rather than undergoing a rigorous workout routine.
