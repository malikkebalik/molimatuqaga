Beyonce dressed up like a Christmas tree for her office holiday party: amazing?

Publish date: 2024-07-31

I never really think of Beyonce as a cutesy kind of woman. Like, does she ever act silly and wear dumb costumes just to make people laugh? She wears costumes and does theme-dressing, of course, but the purpose is usually “sexy.” But here are some photos of Beyonce and Jay-Z leaving an office Christmas party – their office Christmas party, which they hosted – a few nights ago. Beyonce is dressed like a Christmas tree! And it’s cute, not “sexy.” I like her more for this. That she dresses up like this for a Christmas party. It’s adorable.

Beyonce and Jay-Z have had a mixed 2015. They launched Tidal which… is it considered a success? Probably not. They also tried to launch a vegan food delivery service, which we’ve barely heard anything about past the launch. Still, they’re making money and their marriage seems pretty strong these days, unlike the rough patch they had in 2014.

Some people believe that Beyonce is going to drop another surprise album soon. We’re still waiting on Rihanna’s long-gestating album too, and I would think that Beyonce and Rihanna are probably keeping their eyes out for each other’s release schedule. While they’re competitive/friendly, they don’t want to drop their albums within the same few weeks. My guess is that Rihanna will go first, and we’ll have new music from Beyonce… I don’t know, maybe March/April-ish?
