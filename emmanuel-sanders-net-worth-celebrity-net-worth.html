What is Emmanuel Sanders' Net Worth?

Emmanuel Sanders is an American professional football player who has a net worth of $6 million. Emmanuel Sanders was born in Bellville, Texas in March 1987. He is a wide receiver who earned seven All-District honors at Bellville High School. Sanders played his college football at Southern Methodist University where he was All-Conference for his final two seasons. He was drafted #82 by the Pittsburgh Steelers in the 2010 NFL Draft and played for the Steelers from 2010 to 2013.

Sanders was selected to the Pro Bowl in 2014. He won the AFC Championship in 2010 with the Steelers and in 2015 with the Broncos. In 2015 Sanders was ranked #95 in the list of Top 100 Players. In 2014 he signed a three year deal with Denver for $15 million.
